## 1. Art Movements

### Classical & Renaissance

| Movement | Era | Visual Traits | Prompt Keywords |
|----------|-----|---------------|-----------------|
| **Baroque** | 1600-1750 | Dramatic light, rich detail, movement | "baroque", "chiaroscuro", "ornate", "dramatic lighting" |
| **Rococo** | 1700-1800 | Pastel, ornate, playful, curves | "rococo", "pastel", "ornate", "whimsical" |
| **Neoclassical** | 1750-1850 | Order, symmetry, classical themes | "neoclassical", "classical", "symmetrical" |

### Modern Art

| Movement | Era | Visual Traits | Prompt Keywords |
|----------|-----|---------------|-----------------|
| **Impressionism** | 1860-1890 | Light, color, brushstrokes, atmosphere | "impressionist", "soft focus", "painterly", "Monet style" |
| **Post-Impressionism** | 1886-1905 | Bold color, geometric | "post-impressionist", "Van Gogh style", "bold colors" |
| **Expressionism** | 1905-1920 | Distortion, emotion, bold | "expressionist", "distorted", "emotional", "bold" |
| **Cubism** | 1907-1920s | Geometric, fragmented, multiple views | "cubist", "geometric", "fragmented", "Picasso style" |
| **Surrealism** | 1920s-1950s | Dreamlike, impossible, juxtaposition | "surrealist", "dreamscape", "impossible", "Dal√≠ style" |
| **Abstract Expressionism** | 1940s-1950s | Gesture, emotion, non-representational | "abstract expressionist", "gestural", "Pollock style" |

### Contemporary

| Movement | Era | Visual Traits | Prompt Keywords |
|----------|-----|---------------|-----------------|
| **Pop Art** | 1950s-1960s | Bold, commercial, repetition | "pop art", "Warhol style", "bold colors", "commercial" |
| **Minimalism** | 1960s-1970s | Simple, clean, essential | "minimalist", "clean", "simple", "negative space" |
| **Photorealism** | 1970s+ | Hyper-detailed, photograph-like | "photorealistic", "hyper-detailed", "realistic" |
| **Street Art** | 1980s+ | Urban, spray paint, stencil | "street art", "graffiti", "Banksy style", "urban" |
