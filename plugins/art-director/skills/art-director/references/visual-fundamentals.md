## 1. Composition

### Rule of Thirds

Divide frame into 3x3 grid. Place subject on intersections or along lines.

```
┌───┬───┬───┐
│   │   │ ● │  ← Subject on intersection
├───┼───┼───┤
│   │   │   │
├───┼───┼───┤
│   │   │   │
└───┴───┴───┘
```

**Prompt keywords:** "rule of thirds composition", "subject positioned off-center", "asymmetrical balance"

### Golden Ratio (Fibonacci Spiral)

More dynamic than rule of thirds. Subject at spiral's focal point.

**Prompt keywords:** "golden ratio composition", "fibonacci spiral", "dynamic balance"

### Symmetry

Perfect balance. Conveys stability, power, formality.

```
┌─────────────┐
│      │      │
│   ●  │  ●   │
│      │      │
└─────────────┘
```

**Prompt keywords:** "symmetrical composition", "perfect symmetry", "centered", "mirror"

**Famous users:** Wes Anderson, Stanley Kubrick

### Leading Lines

Lines guide viewer's eye toward subject.

| Type | Examples | Effect |
|------|----------|--------|
| Straight | Roads, bridges, fences | Direct, powerful |
| Curved | Rivers, paths, coastlines | Gentle, natural |
| Diagonal | Stairs, shadows | Dynamic, energy |
| Converging | Railway tracks, hallways | Depth, perspective |

**Prompt keywords:** "leading lines toward subject", "converging perspective", "road leading to..."

### Negative Space

Empty space around subject. Creates focus, breathing room.

**Prompt keywords:** "negative space", "minimalist composition", "isolated subject", "clean background"

### Frame Within Frame

Use elements (doorways, windows, arches) to frame subject.

**Prompt keywords:** "framed by doorway", "natural frame", "window frame composition"

### Depth Layers

Foreground → Middle ground → Background

**Prompt keywords:** "layered composition", "foreground interest", "depth layers", "atmospheric perspective"

## 2. Color Theory

### Color Wheel Basics

```
        Yellow
    Green     Orange
  Cyan          Red
    Blue      Magenta
        Violet
```

### Color Harmonies

| Harmony | Relationship | Effect | Example |
|---------|--------------|--------|---------|
| **Complementary** | Opposite on wheel | High contrast, vibrant | Orange + Blue |
| **Analogous** | Adjacent colors | Harmonious, calm | Blue + Cyan + Green |
| **Triadic** | Equidistant (120°) | Balanced, vibrant | Red + Yellow + Blue |
| **Split-complementary** | One + two adjacent to complement | Contrast + harmony | Blue + Yellow-Orange + Red-Orange |
| **Monochromatic** | One hue, vary saturation/value | Unified, moody | Various blues |

### Color Temperature

| Temperature | Colors | Mood | Time of Day |
|-------------|--------|------|-------------|
| **Warm** | Red, Orange, Yellow | Energy, passion, comfort | Golden hour, sunset |
| **Cool** | Blue, Green, Purple | Calm, sadness, mystery | Blue hour, overcast |
| **Neutral** | Gray, Brown, Beige | Stable, natural | Midday, shade |

**Prompt keywords:**
- Warm: "warm golden tones", "sunset palette", "amber and honey colors"
- Cool: "cool blue tones", "icy palette", "cyan and teal"

### Color Psychology

| Color | Associations | Use For |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| **Red** | Passion, danger, energy, love | Action, urgency, romance |
| **Orange** | Warmth, enthusiasm, creativity | Friendly, energetic |
| **Yellow** | Joy, optimism, caution | Happy, attention-grabbing |
| **Green** | Nature, growth, health, envy | Natural, fresh, money |
| **Blue** | Trust, calm, sadness, cold | Corporate, peaceful, melancholy |
| **Purple** | Royalty, mystery, spirituality | Luxury, magic, wisdom |
| **Pink** | Romance, femininity, playful | Soft, romantic, youthful |
| **Black** | Power, elegance, death | Sophisticated, dramatic |
| **White** | Purity, clean, empty | Minimal, clean, innocent |

### Popular Color Grades

| Grade | Description | Prompt Keywords |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Teal & Orange** | Skin pops against cool BG | "teal and orange color grading", "blockbuster look" |
| **Bleach Bypass** | Desaturated, high contrast | "bleach bypass", "silver retention", "gritty" |

## 3. Lighting

### Lighting Direction

```
           BACK
             ↓
      ↙           ↘
   RIM             RIM
     ↖     ●     ↗
       KEY   FILL
             ↑
          FRONT
```

### Portrait Lighting Patterns

| Pattern | Key Light Position | Shadow | Effect |
|---------|---------------------|--------|--------|
| **Butterfly** | Front, high above | Under nose (butterfly shape) | Glamorous, beauty |
| **Loop** | 30-45° side, above eye | Nose shadow loops toward cheek | Natural, flattering |
| **Rembrandt** | 45-60° side, above | Triangle under eye | Dramatic, artistic |
| **Split** | 90° side | Half face dark | Mysterious, dramatic |
| **Broad** | Light on camera-side | Far side dark | Face appears wider |
| **Short** | Light on far side | Camera-side dark | Face appears slimmer |

**Prompt keywords:**
- "Rembrandt lighting with triangle shadow under eye"
- "butterfly lighting, glamour portrait"
- "split lighting, half face in shadow"

### Light Quality

| Quality | Source | Shadow Edge | Feel |
|---------|--------|-------------|------|
| **Hard** | Sun, bare flash, spotlight | Sharp, defined | Dramatic, harsh |
| **Soft** | Overcast, softbox, diffused | Gradual, soft | Gentle, flattering |

**Prompt keywords:**
- Hard: "hard light", "sharp shadows", "direct sunlight"
- Soft: "soft diffused light", "overcast lighting", "gentle shadows"

### Natural Light Conditions

| Time | Color | Quality | Mood |
|------|-------|---------|------|
| **Golden hour** | Warm orange/gold | Soft, directional | Romantic, warm |
| **Blue hour** | Cool blue/purple | Soft, ambient | Moody, mysterious |
| **Midday** | Neutral white | Hard, overhead | Harsh, unflattering |
| **Overcast** | Cool gray | Soft, even | Neutral, soft |
| **Shade** | Cool blue | Soft, even | Natural, open |

**Prompt keywords:**
- "golden hour sunlight, warm glow, long shadows"
- "blue hour twilight, cool ambient light"
- "overcast soft light, even illumination"

### Dramatic Lighting Techniques

| Technique | Description | Prompt Keywords |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Chiaroscuro** | Strong light-dark contrast | "chiaroscuro", "dramatic contrast", "Caravaggio lighting" |
| **Rim/Edge** | Light from behind edges subject | "rim light", "backlit", "edge lighting" |
| **Silhouette** | Subject dark against bright BG | "silhouette", "backlit silhouette" |
| **Low-key** | Mostly dark, selective light | "low-key lighting", "dark and moody" |
| **High-key** | Bright, minimal shadows | "high-key lighting", "bright and airy" |

## 4. Lens & Perspective

### Focal Length Effects

| Focal Length | Category | Characteristics | Best For |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|----------|
| **8-14mm** | Fisheye | Extreme distortion, 180° | Creative, action |
| **14-24mm** | Ultra Wide | Strong distortion, expansive | Architecture, landscape |
| **24-35mm** | Wide | Moderate distortion, context | Street, environmental |
| **35-50mm** | Normal | Natural perspective | Documentary, everyday |
| **50-85mm** | Short Tele | Slight compression, flattering | General portrait |
| **85-135mm** | Portrait | Compression, shallow DOF | Beauty, headshot |
| **200-400mm** | Telephoto | Strong compression | Wildlife, sports |
| **400mm+** | Super Tele | Extreme compression | Distant subjects |

### Compression Effect

**Wide (24mm):** Background appears far, face distorts up close
**Telephoto (200mm):** Background appears close, layers stack

**Prompt keywords:**
- Wide: "wide angle perspective", "expansive", "slight distortion"
- Tele: "telephoto compression", "stacked layers", "compressed perspective"

### Depth of Field

| Aperture | DOF | Look | Use |
|----------|-----|------|-----|
| **f/1.4-2.8** | Very shallow | Creamy bokeh, isolated subject | Portraits, products |
| **f/4-5.6** | Moderate | Some separation | General |
| **f/8-11** | Deep | Most in focus | Landscape, architecture |
| **f/16-22** | Maximum | Everything sharp | Deep focus |

**Prompt keywords:**
- Shallow: "shallow depth of field", "bokeh background", "f/1.4 aperture"
- Deep: "deep focus", "everything in focus", "f/11"

### Special Lens Effects

| Effect | Description | Prompt Keywords |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Bokeh** | Out-of-focus light blobs | "creamy bokeh", "soft background blur" |
| **Anamorphic** | Oval bokeh, horizontal flares | "anamorphic lens", "oval bokeh", "horizontal lens flare" |
| **Tilt-shift** | Selective focus plane | "tilt-shift", "miniature effect" |
| **Lens flare** | Light artifacts | "lens flare", "sun flare" |

## 5. Camera Angles

### Vertical Angles

| Angle | Camera Position | Effect | Use For |
|-------|-----------------|--------|---------|
| **Eye level** | Subject's eye height | Neutral, relatable | Default, conversation |
| **Low angle** | Below, looking up | Power, dominance, heroic | Heroes, authority |
| **High angle** | Above, looking down | Vulnerability, weakness | Victims, overview |
| **Bird's eye** | Directly above | Overview, god-like | Establishing, patterns |
| **Worm's eye** | Ground level up | Extreme power, dramatic | Imposing buildings |

**Prompt keywords:**
- "eye level shot", "low angle looking up", "high angle looking down"
- "bird's eye view", "worm's eye perspective"

### Dutch Angle (Tilted)

Camera rotated on axis. Creates unease, tension, disorientation.

**Prompt keywords:** "dutch angle", "tilted frame", "canted angle"

### Horizon Line Placement

| Placement | Emphasis | Use |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Low horizon** | Sky, height | Dramatic skies, tall subjects |
| **Middle** | Balance | General, neutral |
| **High horizon** | Ground, foreground | Foreground interest, grounding |

## Decision Flowchart

```
What's the subject?
     │
     ├─► Person ──► Portrait lens (85mm), eye-level, Rembrandt lighting
     │
     ├─► Landscape ──► Wide lens (24mm), rule of thirds, golden hour
     │
     ├─► Product ──► Normal lens (50mm), soft light, negative space
     │
     ├─► Action ──► Wide/normal, high shutter, dynamic angle
     │
     └─► Architecture ──► Ultra wide, symmetry or leading lines, midday

What mood?
     │
     ├─► Dramatic ──► Low-key, hard light, complementary colors
     │
     ├─► Peaceful ──► Soft light, analogous colors, symmetry
     │
     ├─► Mysterious ──► Blue hour, rim light, dutch angle
     │
     └─► Joyful ──► Golden hour, warm palette, eye-level
```
